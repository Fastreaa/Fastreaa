## 👨‍💻 About Me

Engineering student at Universidad Iberoamericana focused on building minimalist, high-impact digital experiences and designing robust data architectures. From crafting clean web ecosystems to developing complex data pipelines, I work at the intersection of infrastructure, software, and science communication.

*   🔭 **Currently Building:** Growing **LOVELACE** digital agency with React, Next.js, and Tailwind, focusing on premium, disruptive design.
*   🌌 **Science Comm:** Directing **DESCONOCIDX CIENTIFICX**, sharing science and technology with a community of over 300,000.
*   ⚙️ **Tech Focus:** Cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure), Data Analysis, ETL pipelines, and API integrations.
*   🔬 **Research:** Muon invariant mass analysis with **ROOT/CERN**.
*   🐧 **Environment:** Living in the terminal. Debian + Emacs.
